 # Product Feedback Analysis

## Executive Summary

This project analyzes 23 Google Play Store reviews of the Zomato app to identify recurring customer issues and recommend product improvements.

---

# Top Pain Point 1: Delivery Delays

### Problem

Many users reported:

- Late deliveries
- Incorrect ETA predictions
- Delivery partners handling multiple orders
- Food arriving cold

### Recommendation

Implement an AI-powered Dynamic ETA Prediction System that continuously updates delivery estimates based on real-time traffic and rider location.

**Priority:** High

---

# Top Pain Point 2: Customer Support

### Problem

Users frequently complained about:

- AI chatbot failing to understand issues
- Difficult refund process
- Missing item complaints rejected
- Lack of human support

### Recommendation

Introduce a **"Talk to a Human"** option after the chatbot fails twice to resolve an issue.

**Priority:** High

---

# Top Pain Point 3: Pricing Transparency

### Problem

Users mentioned:

- Platform fees
- Packing charges
- Restaurant prices higher than offline
- Price differences compared to competitors

### Recommendation

Display a detailed price breakdown before checkout, showing restaurant price, taxes, delivery charges, and platform fees separately.

**Priority:** Medium

---

# Positive Feedback

Several users appreciated:

- Easy-to-use interface
- Wide variety of restaurants
- Convenient ordering process
- Fast deliveries in many cases
- Helpful restaurant ratings and reviews

These strengths should be maintained while improving customer pain points.

---

# Conclusion

The analysis indicates that delivery reliability, customer support quality, and pricing transparency are the three most important issues affecting customer satisfaction.

Improving these areas could increase user trust, improve retention, and reduce negative reviews.
