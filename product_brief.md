 # Product Brief - Zomato User Feedback Analysis

## Executive Summary

This project analyzes 23 Google Play Store reviews of the Zomato app to understand customer pain points and recommend product improvements.

---

## Problem Statement

Users appreciate Zomato's convenience but frequently complain about:

- Delivery delays
- Poor customer support
- High prices and platform fees
- Inaccurate live tracking

These issues reduce customer satisfaction and trust.

---

## Target Users

- Students
- Working professionals
- Families
- Frequent food delivery customers

---

## Key Findings

### 1. Delivery Reliability

Users reported:

- Late deliveries
- Wrong ETA
- Food arriving cold
- Riders handling multiple deliveries

**Recommendation**

Improve ETA prediction using real-time rider location and traffic.

Priority: High

---

### 2. Customer Support

Users reported:

- AI chatbot not solving issues
- Refund requests rejected
- Missing item complaints ignored

**Recommendation**

Allow users to connect to a human support agent after chatbot failure.

Priority: High

---

### 3. Pricing Transparency

Users reported:

- High platform fees
- Packing charges
- Restaurant prices higher than offline
- Fewer discounts

**Recommendation**

Show a complete price breakdown before payment.

Priority: Medium

---

## Product Roadmap

Q1
- Human Support Escalation

Q2
- Dynamic ETA Prediction

Q3
- Transparent Price Breakdown

Q4
- Improved Live Order Tracking

---

## Success Metrics

- Reduce delivery complaints by 30%
- Reduce customer support resolution time
- Improve customer satisfaction score
- Increase repeat orders

---

## Expected Business Impact

- Better customer experience
- Higher customer retention
- Increased trust
- Lower refund disputes

---

## Conclusion

The review analysis shows that improving delivery reliability, customer support, and pricing transparency should be Zomato's highest priorities.

Addressing these issues can significantly improve customer satisfaction and long-term retention.
