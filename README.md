# Product Feedback Analysis – Zomato

## Project Overview

This project analyzes user reviews from the Zomato app on the Google Play Store to identify common customer pain points and recommend product improvements.

## Objective

- Analyze customer feedback
- Identify recurring themes
- Prioritize user problems
- Suggest product features
- Demonstrate product management thinking

## Dataset

- Source: Google Play Store Reviews
- Number of Reviews: 23
- Ratings Included:
  - 1 Star
  - 2 Star
  - 3 Star
  - 4 Star
  - 5 Star

The review dataset is available in:

`zomato_reviews.csv`

## Methodology

1. Collected user reviews from Google Play Store.
2. Organized reviews into a CSV file.
3. Categorized reviews into themes.
4. Counted mentions of each theme.
5. Identified top customer pain points.
6. Proposed product improvements.
7. Prioritized features using Impact vs Effort.

## Major Themes

- Delivery Delays
- Poor Customer Support
- Pricing & Extra Charges
- Missing Items
- Live Tracking Issues
- Positive Delivery Experience
- Easy-to-use Interface

## Top Pain Points

### 1. Delivery Delays
Users frequently reported inaccurate ETAs and delayed deliveries.

**Suggested Feature**
- Dynamic ETA prediction using real-time delivery data.

---

### 2. Customer Support
Many users were frustrated with AI-based support and refund handling.

**Suggested Feature**
- One-click escalation from AI chatbot to a human support agent.

---

### 3. Pricing Transparency
Users complained about high platform fees and price differences compared to restaurants.

**Suggested Feature**
- Transparent price breakdown before checkout.

## Success Metrics

- Reduce delivery complaints
- Increase customer satisfaction
- Improve refund resolution rate
- Increase repeat orders
